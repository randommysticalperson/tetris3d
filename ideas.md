## Idea 1: Cyberpunk Neon Arcade

**Design Movement**: Cyberpunk / Retro-futurism arcade aesthetic
**Core Principles**:
1. Glowing neon edges on dark backgrounds create depth and drama
2. High contrast between the dark void and vivid piece colors
3. Scanline and CRT-inspired overlays for retro authenticity
4. Information density — score, next piece, level all visible at a glance

**Color Philosophy**: Deep black/charcoal background (#0a0a0f) with neon-bright piece colors — cyan, magenta, electric yellow, hot pink, lime green. The darkness makes the game feel like an arcade cabinet in a dim room. Each piece type has a signature glow color.

**Layout Paradigm**: Full-screen immersive canvas with floating HUD elements. The 3D well is center-stage, slightly rotated for perspective. Score and next-piece preview float as translucent panels on the sides.

**Signature Elements**:
- Neon glow outlines on every cube face
- Scanline overlay effect across the entire viewport
- Particle burst effects when layers are cleared

**Interaction Philosophy**: Snappy, responsive controls with visual feedback — pieces flash on rotation, ghost pieces show landing position. Keyboard-driven with on-screen touch controls for mobile.

**Animation**: Layer clear causes cubes to shatter into particles that float upward. Pieces have a subtle pulse glow when active. Camera slowly orbits the well.

**Typography System**: Monospace display font (Space Mono or similar) for scores and UI. Bold condensed sans-serif for titles. All text has a subtle glow matching the primary accent color.

## Idea 2: Minimalist Isometric

**Design Movement**: Swiss Design / Isometric minimalism
**Core Principles**:
1. Clean geometric precision — every element serves a purpose
2. Flat colors with subtle ambient occlusion for depth
3. Generous whitespace frames the game as a centerpiece
4. Information hierarchy through scale and position, not decoration

**Color Philosophy**: Warm off-white background (#f5f0eb) with bold, saturated piece colors matching the reference image exactly — yellow, green, red, blue, orange, white, black. Colors are flat with no gradients, relying on face shading (light/medium/dark per face) for 3D effect.

**Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric split — game well occupies 60% left, stats/controls panel on right with generous padding. The well is rendered in true isometric projection for a clean, architectural feel.

**Signature Elements**:
- Crisp black outlines on every cube (1px)
- Isometric grid lines visible on the well floor
- Subtle drop shadows beneath the well

**Interaction Philosophy**: Precise and deliberate. No unnecessary flourishes. Controls are clearly labeled with keyboard shortcuts visible. Ghost piece shown as wireframe outline.

**Animation**: Smooth linear translations — no bouncing or easing overshoot. Layer clears cause rows to fade out cleanly. Minimal but purposeful motion.

**Typography System**: Helvetica Neue / Inter for body, a geometric display font like Archivo Black for the title and score. Numbers use tabular figures for alignment.
</idea>

## Idea 3: Deep Space Observatory

**Design Movement**: Sci-fi observatory / Space station control panel
**Core Principles**:
1. The game well is a containment field floating in space
2. UI elements feel like holographic instrument panels
3. Depth is created through parallax starfield and atmospheric fog
4. Pieces feel like they have physical weight and material properties

**Color Philosophy**: Deep space navy (#080c1a) with subtle blue-purple nebula gradients in the far background. Piece colors are vivid and slightly metallic — each piece has specular highlights and ambient reflections. UI text in cool blue-white (#c8d8ff) with accent highlights in warm amber (#ffb347).

**Layout Paradigm**: Full-viewport immersive 3D scene. The tetris well floats in the center with a subtle perspective camera. HUD elements are positioned as floating holographic panels with slight transparency and blur backdrop.

**Signature Elements**:
- Animated starfield parallax behind the game
- Holographic grid lines on the well walls that pulse subtly
- Atmospheric fog/glow at the bottom of the well

**Interaction Philosophy**: Pieces feel weighty — slight delay and momentum on movement. Camera subtly shifts based on piece position. Satisfying "lock" sound and visual when pieces place.

**Animation**: Pieces have smooth gravity with slight acceleration. Layer clears trigger a horizontal energy wave that dissolves the row. Camera has gentle idle sway. Stars slowly drift.

**Typography System**: Rajdhani or Orbitron for display/headers (technical, angular). Source Sans Pro for readable body text. All text has a subtle outer glow for readability against dark backgrounds.
</idea>
